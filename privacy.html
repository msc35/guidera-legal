2. What Data We Collect

We collect and process the minimum data necessary to provide the app's features. We categorize this data as follows:

2.1 Location Data

We access your device's precise GPS location to display your position on the map, find nearby places, provide walking directions during tours, and enable Walkman mode (background location for continuous narration). Your location data is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored on any server we control. Coarse location context (such as city or neighborhood names) may be included in requests to our AI providers to generate relevant descriptions.

2.2 User Queries and Content

When you generate an AI description, ask a follow-up question, or create a custom tour, the text of your request — along with place names and contextual information — is sent to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini API, OpenAI API) for processing. These providers process your request and return a response; we do not store your queries on any server we control. Generated descriptions are cached locally on your device for offline access.

2.3 Advertising Data

For free-tier users, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK integrated in the app may collect device identifiers (including the Identifier for Advertisers, or IDFA, if you grant permission), IP address, and ad interaction data. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Premium users see no advertisements and no advertising data is collected.

2.4 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, process, or have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.). We store only the expiry date of your premium pass locally on your device to determine your subscription status.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information; photos, videos, or biometric data; browsing history outside the app; contacts or address book data; health or fitness data; or any personally identifiable information. The app has no user accounts, no registration, and no login.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may receive data from the app. Each service processes data according to its own privacy policy:

4.1 Google Gemini API

Used for generating AI descriptions, follow-up Q&A, and tour content. Receives place names, user queries, and contextual information (such as city name, selected persona, language). Does not receive your precise GPS coordinates. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 OpenAI API

Used for text-to-speech audio generation. Receives only the text of AI-generated descriptions — no personal information, location data, or user identifiers. Per OpenAI's API data usage policy, data sent via the API is not used to train their models. OpenAI's privacy policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4.3 Google AdMob

Used to display banner advertisements and rewarded video ads to free-tier users. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and ad interaction data. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we request consent before serving personalized ads in compliance with the Google EU User Consent Policy. If you deny consent or deny the App Tracking Transparency prompt, you will receive non-personalized ads. Premium users see no ads. Google AdMob privacy: AdMob & Privacy

4.4 Apple Frameworks

We use Apple MapKit (maps and directions), StoreKit (in-app purchases), CoreLocation (GPS), ActivityKit (Live Activity), and WatchConnectivity (Apple Watch). Data processed by these frameworks is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Transfers Outside the EU

When you use features that require AI processing or when ads are served, data may be transmitted to servers located in the United States operated by Google and OpenAI. These transfers are necessary to provide the app's core functionality. Both Google and OpenAI maintain data processing agreements and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure an adequate level of data protection as required by the GDPR. No personal data is stored on these servers beyond the time needed to process and return your request.

6. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing Google AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized advertising. You may deny this request at any time, and you can change your preference in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Tracking. Denying tracking does not affect app functionality — you will simply receive non-personalized ads.

7. GDPR Consent for Advertising (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we will present a consent dialog before serving ads, in compliance with the Google EU User Consent Policy and the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF). You may choose to consent to or decline personalized advertising. You can change your consent preferences at any time through the app's Settings.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not operate a backend server, we do not retain any of your personal data. All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

Using the "Reset App" or "Delete All My Data" option in the app's Settings, which clears all cached descriptions, audio files, tour progress, custom tours, and preferences; or by deleting the app from your device, which removes all associated data from your device's storage.

Data processed by third-party services (Google, OpenAI) is subject to their respective retention policies. API requests are processed transiently and are not stored permanently by these providers.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed by the app. API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain, which provides hardware-backed encryption. All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption. Locally stored data is protected by iOS's built-in data protection (file-level encryption tied to your device passcode). However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Guidera is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at mehmetselim692@icloud.com and we will take steps to remove such information. Note: because the app does not collect personal information or require accounts, there is no mechanism through which a child could provide us with personal data directly.

Under the CCPA (California residents):

You have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

Because Guidera does not collect personal information, does not have user accounts, and stores all data locally on your device, most of these rights are already satisfied by the app's design. You can exercise your right to erasure at any time by using the "Reset App" feature in Settings or by deleting the app.

12. Background Location Usage

When you activate Walkman mode during a guided tour, the app uses your location in the background to provide continuous narration as you walk between tour stops, detect arrival at the next stop, and update the Live Activity on your Lock Screen with distance and route information. Background location access is used solely for active tour navigation and stops when the tour ends. You can revoke background location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Guidera → Location → While Using the App.
